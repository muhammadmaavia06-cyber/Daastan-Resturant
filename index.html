Our Story

A Legacy Forged
in Spice & Soul

Dastaan was born from a single conviction: that Pakistani cuisine, in all its layered complexity, deserves a stage as grand as the culture it springs from. Founded in 1998 by Chef Nasir Ali in the heart of Karachi, our kitchen became a sanctuary where ancestral recipes met studied mastery.

From the slow-simmered depths of a Beef Nihari to the fragrant elevation of a Pulao crowned in saffron, every plate that leaves our kitchen carries the weight of a story — of soil, of family, of fire. We call it Dastaan. Because great food is never just food. It is memory, made edible.
